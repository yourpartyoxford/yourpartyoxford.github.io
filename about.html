ABOUT

We are a Your Party proto-branch based in East Oxford and incorporating members from all parts of Oxfordshire.

Meetings

Oxford Town Hall

Our main assembly is at the Oxford Town Hall on St Aldates on the third Tuesday of the month. All members (and non-members!) from Oxford and surroundings are welcome.

Botley Socialist Alliance

BSA meets at Seacourt Hall in Botley, normally held on the second Wednesday of the month. Many of the attendees are also members of Your Party.

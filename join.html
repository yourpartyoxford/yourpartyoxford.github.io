Meetings

Oxford Town Hall

Our main assembly is at the Oxford Town Hall on St Aldates from 7pm on the third Tuesday of the month. All members (and non-members!) from Oxford and surroundings are welcome.

Botley Socialist Alliance

BSA meetings are from 7.30pm at Seacourt Hall in Botley, normally held on the second Wednesday of the month. Many of the attendees are also members of Your Party.
